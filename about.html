ABOUT US

ABOUT OUR COLLEGE

PERI Institute of Technology (PERI IT) was founded in 2010 by the PERI Educational and Charitable Trust with the goal of providing top-notch technical education. The institute is proud to have NAAC Accreditation and has been granted autonomous status starting from the academic year 2024-2025. Nestled in a 15-acre campus in Mannivakkam, Chennai, PERI IT offers an enriching learning environment with smart classrooms, advanced laboratories, transport facilities, a cafeteria, and a well-stocked library. The institute provides undergraduate (B.E.) and postgraduate (M.E.) programs designed to foster academic excellence while encouraging co-curricular and extracurricular growth for students.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Established in 2011, the Departments of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) and in 2010 Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) are dedicated to shaping skilled professionals. The ECE department offers one undergraduate (UG) program and two postgraduate (PG) programs—M.E. in Applied Electronics and M.E. in Communication Systems. The EEE department offers one UG program and one PG program—M.E. in Power Electronics & Drives. Both departments have a team of passionate and experienced faculty members who are committed to equipping students with the latest knowledge and technical expertise in their respective fields.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

This conference aims to be a hub for research and innovation in Electronics and Communication Engineering and Electrical and Electronics Engineering. It provides an opportunity for researchers, academicians, industry leaders, and students to share their ideas, discoveries, and technological advancements. The event is carefully planned to bring together renowned speakers, panel discussions, and networking opportunities that encourage collaboration and meaningful conversations. Participants will get a platform to showcase their research, gain insights from experts, and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
